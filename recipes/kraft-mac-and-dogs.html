Kraft Mac and Dogs

Description

Simple: Kraft Mac and Cheese mixed with boiled hot dog slices. Not the healthiest, but it tastes good!

Ingredients

Yields 2 servings and takes approximately 20-30 minutes to make this meal, 30 minutes to prep and 30 minutes to cook.

  • 1 box of Kraft Mac and Cheese
  • 6 cups water and 2 cups of water
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 4 tbsp butter (1/2 a stick)
  • 2 hot dogs
  • salt and pepper to taste (optional)

Steps:

  1. Put some salt in the 6 cup water pot.
  2. Boil 6 cups of water and 2 cups of water on separate stovetops.
  3. When 2 cups of water boil, put the two hot dogs in the pot and turn the heat down to medium. Let them cook/simmer for 4-5 minutes and then take them out.
  4. When 6 cups of water boil, pour in the noodles and let them cook for 7-8 minutes while ocassionally stirring.
  5. Chop the hot dogs into thin slices while the noodles are cooking.
  6. Once done cooking, drain the water from the noodles with a strainer and put them back in the pot (and also make sure you've moved your pot to a non-hot stovetop).
  7. Add milk and butter to the noodles and stir. Then add the cheese powder and stir. Finally, add the hot dog slices and stir.
  8. Add salt and pepper to taste, spoon into bowls, and serve!
