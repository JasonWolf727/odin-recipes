Sticky Salmon Rice Bowl

Description

"Nothing packs in flavor, nutrients, and protein quite like a sticky salmon rice bowl. And when it’s filled with so many delicious ingredients (think salmon, cucumber, broccoli, soy sauce, honey, lime juice, garlic…) you can’t help but love it.

This meal is made up of a few different parts, all of which come together to create a flavor-packed and extremely satisfying bowl. The recipe yields 4 servings, making it fantastic for feeding the family, enjoying as dinner and next-day lunch for two, or preparing for the week’s meal prep!"

-Excerpt taken from erinliveswhole's description of the meal

Ingredients

Yields 4 servings and takes approximately an hour to make this meal, 30 minutes to prep and 30 minutes to cook.

  • 2lbs salmon (skin off)
  • 2 cups uncooked rice
  • 3 baby cucumbers (optional)
  • 1 large head broccoli, chopped into small florets

Cucmbers (optional):

  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Juice of 1 lime

Sticky sauce:

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp chili crunch/red pepper flake
  • 1 tsp arrowroot powder (sub cornstarch)
  • 2 tbsp water

Spicy mayo:

  • 1/2 cup mayo
  • 1 tbsp siracha
  • 2-3 tbsp water

Steps:

  1. Prepare rice according to package. Set aside.
  2. *Slice cucumbers. In a bowl, whisk together sesame oil, soy sauce, honey, chili crunch, and lime juice. Add cucumbers and mix until well coated. Set aside.
  3. Chop salmon into bite size chunks. Toss with oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Lay flat in the air fryer and fry 7 minutes at 400F. Alternatively bake 10 minutes at 425.
  4. In a sauce pan, make the sauce. Over medium-high heat, add soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, sesame oil, grated ginger, garlic, and chili crunch. In a separate little dish, whisk together arrowroot and water. Once sauce starts to bubble, add arrowroot slurry.
  5. Let sauce bubble and cook for 2-3 minutes or until thickening. It will also thicken more as it cools.
  6. Steam broccoli until fork tender.
  7. Make spicy mayo by mixing all ingredients together.
  8. Add rice to shallow bowl or plate, then add cooked broccoli, marinated cucumbers, and cooked salmon. Pour sticky sauce over top of salmon, coating well. Top with spicy mayo and sesame seeds.
